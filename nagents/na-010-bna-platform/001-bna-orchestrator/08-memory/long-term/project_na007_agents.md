na-007-bnprs-cxo is the CXO agent group. Created 2026-05-27.

Named after the five Pandava brothers from the Mahabharata:

- 001 aid-ceo — Yudhisthira — Chief Executive Officer — strategy, vision, governance, stakeholder leadership
- 002 aid-cto — Bhima — Chief Technology Officer — technology strategy, engineering, platform, security
- 003 aid-cpo — Arjuna — Chief Product Officer — product strategy, roadmap, UX/CX, competitive intel
- 004 aid-cfo — Nakula — Chief Financial Officer — finance, treasury, compliance, investor relations
- 005 aid-coo — Sahadeva — Chief Operating Officer — operations, delivery, process excellence

Pandava council quorum: min 3 of 5 CXOs for major decisions; Yudhisthira (CEO) holds the casting vote.
Next available code: 006.

**Why:** Dedicated AI agents for each CXO function, distinct from na-008-bnprs-team (employee AIDs) and na-002-bnprs-core (business ops). The Pandava naming provides memorable identifiers for the five core executive roles.

**How to apply:** When user works with any CXO agent, load their `03-nucleus/CLAUDE.md` for context. Cross-agent references: CEO→Board, CTO↔CPO (roadmap), CTO↔COO (capacity), CFO↔CEO (board), CFO↔COO (opex).
